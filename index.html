Electric cruiser board

Eb-board is an electric skateboard company located in Santa Barbara, California. Our mission is to provide a lighter, more compact, and more affordable electric skateboard. Eb-boards are powered by an electric motor adding a whole new experience to skateboarding. With the help of this technology, our riders can speed up or brake whenever they need. We hope you will enjoy it!

eb-cruiser

eb-cruiser is a cruiser board which allows its rider to ride long distances with the help of an electric motor. The EB-cruiser is lighter, more compact and more competitively priced than all competitor boards. This cruiser board is ideal for riding in the city. This innovative way of transportation will help you get from point A to point B faster, quicker, and having fun doing it ! Eb-cruiser is addictive.
